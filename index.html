ABOUT ME:

I graduate in 2023 from the University of Manchester with a BSc in Biology with Science and Society. This course gave me a traditional biological education with a unique historical, sociological and political perspective. I focused heavily on human healthcare, studying virology, vaccines and epidemics before specialising in neurotechnology, neuropharmacology and mental healthcare frameworks in the UK. Throughout my studies at Manchester, I built planning and organisational skills whilst exploring science communication through written and audio-visual mediums. This culminated in my final project where I created a video lecture about the ethical issues surrounding neurotechnology development.

I have 2 years experience in the hospitality industry, working in a members restaurant and a high-volume bar, and over 4 years experience as a sports coach, teaching gymnastics and tennis to children from ages 3-16. Both roles have given me excellent customer service skills and confidence in communicating to different demographics. My aim for 2023 is to gain experience in a medical setting, refining my administration and professional skills in order to progress to a career in clinical research.
